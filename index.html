Hiking in Skrapar: Explore Nature & the Pirogoshi Cave

Join us for an unforgettable hiking journey through the stunning landscapes of Skrapar, home to Albania’s most breathtaking mountains, canyons, and the legendary Pirogoshi Cave. At Skrapar Adventures, we’re passionate about delivering safe, exciting, and eco-friendly hiking experiences that connect you with nature and local culture. Whether you’re a casual walker or an experienced explorer, we offer trails and adventures tailored just for you.

  • Hiking in Skrapar: Trek through scenic trails, majestic mountains, and unspoiled nature.
  • Pirogoshi Cave: Discover the wonders of one of Albania’s largest and most fascinating caves.
  • Cultural Experience: Meet locals, taste traditional food, and learn about Skrapar’s history.
  • Eco-Tourism: Explore Albania’s wild beauty while protecting the environment.

Canyon Hiking Adventure

Skrapar’s canyons offer a thrilling hiking experience through wild landscapes, steep cliffs, and clear waters — a hidden gem for nature lovers in southern Albania.

Discovering Pirogoshi Cave

Pirogoshi Cave offers an exciting underground adventure in Albania. Visitors can explore its cool chambers filled with impressive stalactites and stalagmites, experiencing the natural beauty carved over thousands of years. It’s a unique destination for nature lovers and explorers alike.
