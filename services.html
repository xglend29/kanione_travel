Hiking and Tubing Through Breathtaking Canyons

It’s perfect for beginners and adventure lovers alike – whether you're looking to discover Albania’s wild canyons, cool off in crystal-clear waters, or just enjoy an unforgettable outdoor experience.

This tour takes place in Çorovodë, Skrapar, known for its dramatic landscapes and pristine nature. Pirogoshi Cave Adventure

Pirogoshi Cave Adventure

Pirogoshi Cave Exploration is a fascinating underground journey through Albania’s largest cave, located in the wild and beautiful region of Skrapar. This tour is perfect for nature lovers, curious explorers, and those who enjoy combining a light physical challenge with incredible natural sights. No previous experience is needed – just a sense of adventure!

The cave is situated near Çorovodë, Skrapar, and features majestic rock formations, narrow tunnels, underground halls, and small natural pools carved over thousands of years. The experience is guided by professionals who will lead you safely through the cave while sharing stories, geological facts, and local legends.

Duration: approx. 2.5–3 hours

Included: helmet with headlamp, gloves, local guide, transfer, photos, and free parking.

What to bring: comfortable clothing, sturdy hiking shoes with good grip, a light jacket or hoodie (temperature inside the cave is cool), a small backpack, and water.
